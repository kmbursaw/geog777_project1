Nitrate and Cancer Rates in Wisconsin

To perform a linear regression on the cancer rates in Wisconsin by nitrate levels recorded in wells, enter a decay coefficient greater than 0, and a hexbin area from 6-90 square miles. The resulting hexbins will appear on the screen. Additionally, the regression equation fit will appear below, as well as an R Squared value. The closer R Squared is to 1, the better the model fit. To help with the visualization, the user can right click and hold to pan the view down to see a 3D representation of the hexbin levels.
